Why?

To Give World Class Co-Curriculars

In modern times schools need to emphasise on Co-Curriculars to make students effective and impactful. A restructured framework is to have a bigger role of Co-Curriculars without disrupting the core curriculum boundaries.

How?

Taking School Clubs Online

WingXP builds a network of experts (local and remote) that help schools to offer Co-Curriculars of choice, interest, and flair to its students. It does so by creating an online club framework called 'iclubs' for connecting the experts to the students using the power of technology.

What?

A Network of Experts

WingXP offers ready to use expert-led online school clubs for the new age students. These are called WingXP iclubs. Schools with or without existing club structure can use them freely. WingXP opens the same framework to the schools for taking their own school clubs online for free.
