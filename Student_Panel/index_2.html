Club Coordinator

Nidhi Jain

Hi students!!! The topic of the month is Internet of Things. The reason we have chosen this topic is as it is expected to be the next big thing in technology…. Information flow, commands and actions. Many people usualy confuse internet of things with “The Internet of Things (IoT) is a system of interrelated computing devices, mechanical and digital machines, objects, animals or people that are provided with unique identifiers and the ability to transfer data over a network without requiring human-to-human or human-to-computer interaction.” This month we would be trying to understand Internet of things, some small experiments that exhibit the concept, How it is being planned to be used by the industries of all kinds, learn a few terms around internet of things. We have tried to make the learning simple and are sharing the best efforts unabridged as given by the original creator.
